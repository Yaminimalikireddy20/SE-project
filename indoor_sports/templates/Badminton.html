Description of Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced sport played by two players (singles) or four players (doubles) who use rackets to hit a shuttlecock over a net. The objective is to score points by landing the shuttlecock in the opponent's court. Badminton requires agility, precision, and excellent reflexes, making it an exciting and challenging sport to play and watch.

It is one of the most popular sports globally, especially in Asia, and is a staple in the Olympics. The sport promotes cardiovascular fitness, improves hand-eye coordination, and enhances flexibility and endurance.
